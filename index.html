Despite the steady decline in family size, according to the 2012 census, average American families still have 2 children per household. Many children has more than 2 activities after school. The parents are reluctantly spending many hours chauffeuring children all over the town until all the activities are done for the day. Carpooling for children's activities is a must for many parents, expecially for working parents who needs the transportation means for their children after school. The most carpool scheduling is done by email exchanges and phone calls, rather ineffiently, resulting a list of drivers and their driving dates. Some may opt to use a sign up software program but it is not cater to the carpooling needs for the parents.

Introducing Carpool Buddies!

The integrated solution for carpool scheduling and follow-up cares to assist busy parents:
  • Simple way to create carpooling events/activities.
  • Automaticlly send invitation to the other carpooling familes and gather carpooling preference from everyone.
  • Based on everyone's preference, generate a carpool schedule. Everyone has a chance to review and update the carpool schedule until approved.
  • Once it's approved, generate the best carpool routing and option for navigation.
  • An email reminder to drivers and riders. An optional SMS or voice message reminder available. This option can be used for older kids with their mobile devices to remind them to get ready to be picked up!
  • In case of emergency (i.e. flat tire, unexpected traffic jam), send an optional SMS or voice message to drivers and riders for any last-minute alerts.
